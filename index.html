Forge Your Legend

On a fateful day, the Sword of Will goes missing from the Sacred Altar, stripping the human Kingdom of its strength. The hellish monsters from the surrounding lands become emboldened, marching out of the darkness and attacking our villages. There had been a Prophecy of old that this day would come. That a knight would rise and quell this darkness. Many brave warriors have ventured out, fighting back the monsters, in search of the Sword of Will…but none have returned. As you work, forging armour for the Kingdom, you meet a Stranger in the wilderness that commissions you for a dangerous quest.

Core Features

Blacksmithing

Knight Foretold has a fully modular armour system, allowing you to create and equip a variety of armour and gear sets. Blacksmithing requires materials like iron, steel, leather, and more. To learn to make higher level gear players improve their proficiency and equipment, while also finding Blacksmith Plans throughout the game world. These are the long-lost armour designs of renowned Blacksmiths from ages passed.

Combat

The combat system is visceral and dynamic with a repertoire of different attacks that inflict your might on the enemy. Attacks range from running-jump attacks, Bashing your shield into enemy faces, and spartan kicks. The combat also adjusts the impact according to the player's speed and strength, hitting enemies harder and dealing more damage.

Multiplayer

Step into epic medieval battles where teamwork defines victory. Join forces with allies to siege castles, defend strongholds, and clash steel in intense real-time combat. Choose your role, master your weapons, and coordinate strategies to dominate the battlefield and forge your legend.
